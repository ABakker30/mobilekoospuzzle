# HDR Placeholder Files

For development, you can download HDR environments from:
- HDR Haven: https://hdrihaven.com/
- Poly Haven: https://polyhaven.com/hdris

Recommended HDR files:
- studio_1k.hdr (low-res, ~500KB)
- studio_4k.hdr (high-res, ~4MB)
- outdoor_1k.hdr (low-res, ~500KB)  
- outdoor_4k.hdr (high-res, ~4MB)
- sunset_1k.hdr (low-res, ~500KB)
- sunset_4k.hdr (high-res, ~4MB)

The system will gracefully handle missing files and fall back to basic materials.
